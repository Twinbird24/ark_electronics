Below is a list of services that we offer. We service both desktops and laptops. If you cannot find a service that matches your needs then please contact us and we will resolve your problem.

  • Malware removal and protection
    Description: Symptoms of an infection include slow-start up, random restart, malfunctioning software, slow internet, pop-ups, etc. We will remove the malware on your computer that is causing these problems and get your PC functioning normally again.
  • Software help
    Description: Operating system upgrades and reinstalls, data migration, start-up problems, corrupt/ malfunctioning software, all sorts of error messages resolved (blue-screens, etc.)
  • Hardware installation and removal
    Description: Removal and installation of hard drives, video cards, DVD drives, etc.
  • PC cleaning
    Description: Dust, dirt, and other debris can build up inside your PC and may eventually cause a short-circuit and clog up heat sinks and fans. We will clear out this debris.
  • Software installation and removal
    Description: Installation of virus protection software and any other programs and removal of programs that may not be fully removed by the software uninstall wizard.
  • Custom builds
    Description: Have your own PC custom built to match your needs and personality! Gaming PC, office workstation, etc.

If a replacement part must be purchased in order to fix your computer, the price of the replacement part will be added to the $65 repair fee.

Please note: We will not charge you anything if we cannot fix your computer. We charge a flat rate of $65 for a repair. We offer a 30 day limited warranty on most repairs (labour only).
